Who I am

I am a PostDoctoral Researcher at at INFN and University of Padova.
My research interests are cosmology and the large-scale structure of the Universe. I believe that the most intriguing open questions in physics and cosmology concern the origin and evolution of our Universe and the matter-energy components driving its evolution: its dark components, such as dark energy and dark matter, neutrinos, the nature of gravity, etc.
I am involved in the Euclid mission of the European Space Agency as an active member of the Galaxy Clustering Science Working Group (GC-SWG).

Research

Cosmic void statistics to probe the Universe.
